Important to notice

The CinemaPress Movie Database is the only freely available information about all the movies in the world that can be obtained from TheMovieDB and similar API services. Physically, movies are stored on public and private video hosting and the site owner decides which ones to use and which ones to disable. We do not encourage users to ignore the owners of rights and strongly recommend them to enable automatic deletion of protected films by the system.
